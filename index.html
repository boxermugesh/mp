About Criminal Face Identification

Our system uses AI-powered face detection to recognize and match faces against a database of known criminals and missing persons. It assists law enforcement agencies in real-time surveillance, improving public safety, and finding missing individuals quickly.

Equipped with deep learning and neural network models, the system can scan live camera feeds, detect multiple faces simultaneously, and alert authorities when a match is found. It ensures faster investigations and safer communities.

  • ✅ Missing Person Detection
  • ✅ Real-Time Surveillance
  • ✅ Public Area Monitoring
